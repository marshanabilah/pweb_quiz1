Surabaya

I live in this city since I was born. Surabaya has changed so much.

The city is known as Kota Pahlawan “city of heroes” due to the importance of the Battle of Surabaya in galvanizing Indonesian and international support for Indonesian independence during the Indonesian National Revolution.

Although there isn't so many tourist attraction here, there are so many delicious foods and green spaces in Surabaya.

Local Foods

Surabaya has so many delicious local foods. My favorite is Mie Ayam and Nasi Goreng. Surabaya people loves sambal, petis and terasi, it's very common to have that ingredients in local foods.
